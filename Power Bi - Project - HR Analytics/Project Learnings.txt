

Project Learnings
* Identified key factors to reduce attrition
* Improved the hiring process
* Improve needed in employee satisfaction by making sure involvement in the job is as uniform as possible
* Majority of attrition are of the employee aged between 26 – 35 in their 1st year in the company.
* Introduction of some employee bonus or ESOP type schemes to be given after completion of 2-3 years to improve retention
